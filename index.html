Lessons for Monzo Spain
01
Own the human support layer
What Revolut shows Compliance runs on automated flags. At 6M Spanish users a 1% false-positive rate = 60,000 people locked out with no Spanish phone line. Architectural, not fixable.

02

What Revolut shows 1,500 employees, 50 ATMs, €4B revenue, yet still can't process Social Security, no BNPL in Spain, no mortgages. The lending roadmap is vaporware.

What Monzo does Zero hidden markups, zero weekend surcharges, zero "premium for basic features". Treat pricing integrity as core product.

Lessons for Monzo Spain
01
Hard-code BdE + 6pp into every credit product
What WiZink shows STS 149/2020 reinterpreted a 1908 usury law: 26.82% APR retroactively illegal. STS 258/2023 set the rule: BdE average + 6pp. Any revolving/overdraft/Flex above this line = mass litigation.
What Monzo does Pricing governance for every Spanish credit product owns the BdE category baseline. Above BdE + 6pp requires executive sign-off.
02
Brand equity is defensibility
What WiZink shows A PE roll-up of card portfolios acquired via airport stands and mall kiosks. No community, no loyalty. When the usury ruling hit, nobody defended them. Arriaga and Reclamador industrialised the claims.
What Monzo does Build brand affection before you need it. In Spain a defenceless brand during a crisis becomes the category cautionary tale.
03
Plan for Spain's industrialised claims ecosystem
What WiZink shows "Reclamar WiZink" became a household phrase. €81M in revolving provisions in 2024 alone. Losses keep widening despite rate adjustments. The claims tail outlasts the product pivot.
What Monzo does Spanish pre-contractual/contractual documentation is a launch-blocker. Proactive OCU and FACUA relationships from day 1. Higher-than-UK litigation reserves baked in.

Lessons for Monzo Spain

Cede the investing wallet. Own the primary account.
What TR shows Spanish primary-bank NPS 10-20 vs 35-45 for investing. Users say "bueno para cash, no como banco principal". TR is entrenched as the investing app. The rewiring is fighting physics.

A price war acquires mercenaries, not customers
What TR shows Users came for €1 trades and 2% TAE. When competitors match, they leave in 5 minutes. Revolut, N26 and Wise are already closing the gap. Optimised for acquisition, not retention.
What Monzo does Compete on product and service depth, not on the headline number. Trust compounds; a rate hook doesn't.
03

What TR shows Broker (2019) → 4% account (2023) → full bank with Spanish IBAN (2025). Three positionings in five years. The narrative whiplash breaks brand equity and Spanish NPS.
What Monzo does One Spanish story — "your everyday money with Bizum, salary, international life" — for 24 months minimum. Every feature slots into it, never replaces it.
